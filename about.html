Who We Are

Turing's Lab is a software quality engineering company with a single obsession: helping engineering teams ship reliable software faster through the intelligent application of automation and AI.

Our Story

Why Turing's Lab Exists

We've seen firsthand what happens when quality is treated as an afterthought. Releases delayed. Production incidents at 2 AM. Enterprise clients churning because of preventable bugs. Engineering teams demoralized by endless firefighting.

We started Turing's Lab because we believed there was a better way — a systematic, AI-enhanced approach to quality that could give engineering teams back their confidence and velocity.

We named the company after Alan Turing — a scientist who fundamentally changed how we think about what machines can do. Our mission is to apply that same spirit of rigorous thinking to the problem of software quality.

🎯

Our Mission

To make reliable software delivery the default state for every engineering team we work with — regardless of their size, stack, or starting point.

🔭

Our Vision

A world where "it works in production" is not a milestone — it's a baseline. Where quality is engineered in, not bolted on as an afterthought.

🤖

Our Approach

AI-augmented quality engineering. We combine deep QA expertise with cutting-edge automation tooling and machine learning to deliver outcomes no traditional QA service can match.
